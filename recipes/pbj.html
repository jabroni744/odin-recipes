Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

How to make and Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Grape Jelly

Steps

  1. Take 2 Pieces of Bread and spread Peanut Butter on both sides
  2. Spread Grape Jelly on one of the pieces of bread
  3. Sandwich the pieces of bread together. Eat and enjoy!
