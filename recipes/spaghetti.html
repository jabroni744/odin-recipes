Spaghetti with Meat Sauce

How to make and Spaghetti with Meat Sauce

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles/li>
  • Prego Traditional Sauce
  • 1lb of Hamburger

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil
  2. Add spaghetti to boiling water and boil for 10 minutes
  3. While the spaghetti boils begin browning hamburger
  4. Strain the spaghetti
  5. Add Prego sauce to the hamburger when it is finished browning and heat up
  6. Add spagetti to plate and then add meat sauce. Eat and Enjoy!
