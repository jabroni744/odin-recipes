BBQ Nachos

How to make Josh's BBQ Nachos

Ingredients

  • 1 Bag of Tortilla Chips
  • 1 Can of Refried Beans
  • 1 lb of Colby Jack Shredded Cheese
  • 1 lb of Habenaro Shredded Cheese
  • 1 container of premade bbq chicken
  • 1 Jar of Jalepenos
  • 1 Jar of Salsa
  • 16 oz of Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Spread 1 Layer of tortilla chips out on oven safe tray
  3. Sprinkle Colby Jack cheese and Habenaro Cheese
  4. Add BBQ Chicken wherever you want it
  5. Add Refried beans
  6. Add another Layer of chips and repeat steps 3-5
  7. Place tray in oven for 8 minutes
  8. Remove from oven and serve with salsa, jalepenos, and sour cream. Enjoy
